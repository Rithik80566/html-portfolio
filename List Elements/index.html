Rithik's Receipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingrdients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoon yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg Plus 1 yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cup's bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/4 table spoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions
